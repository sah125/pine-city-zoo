LION

The lion (Panthera leo) is the second-largest cat species in the world and the only cats that live in social groups called prides. Our African lions are majestic symbols of strength and courage.

Male lions can weigh up to 550 pounds (250 kg) with their iconic manes growing darker with age. Lionesses do most of the hunting, working together to take down prey.

A lion's roar can be heard up to 5 miles (8 km) away! They spend about 20 hours per day resting to conserve energy for hunting.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM
