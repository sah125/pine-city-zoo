PANDA

The giant panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca) is a bear native to south-central China. These iconic black-and-white bears are actually carnivores that evolved to eat almost exclusively bamboo.

Pandas spend 10-16 hours daily eating up to 40 pounds of bamboo. Their "thumb" is actually an extended wrist bone that helps them grip bamboo stalks.

Newborn pandas weigh only 3-5 ounces - about 1/900th of their mother's weight! Our pandas are part of an international conservation effort to protect this vulnerable species.
